Flowers are the base of all potions. Some grow everywhere, while others are rare. The witch must click or wait for them to grow. Special flowers appear only with spells or upgrades. Rare flowers are needed to craft the most powerful potions.
Potions increase the witch’s abilities. Each recipe requires specific ingredients. Some potions provide speed or money. Others offer powerful temporary effects. The player can choose to use them or sell them. Advanced potions unlock new recipes and areas.
Items improve the witch’s work. A broom allows access to new flowers. Cats automatically collect resources. Some spells speed up production or increase potion value. Buying and upgrading items is essential to progress quickly.
