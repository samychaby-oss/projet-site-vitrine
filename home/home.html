Mystflora

A peaceful idle game like no other.

Discover the universe

A little apprentice witch lived in the heart of a magical forest.

Every day, she wandered through the woods to gather flowers with extraordinary powers.

With these flowers, she prepared unique and delicious potions. Her potions were so powerful that villagers came from far away to buy them.

But her path was not easy, and she had to overcome many challenges. Through courage and magic, she learned to master all kinds of potions.

Help this witch become the greatest witch of all time.

Gameplay

The player embodies a young witch who gathers flowers by clicking on the central flower. These flowers are used to craft potions, which can then be sold to earn money. This money is used to buy upgrades that increase flower collection and speed up potion creation.

As the game progresses, the player unlocks a broom to obtain rarer flowers, familiars like cats that gather resources automatically, and spells that boost production. Even when offline, the familiars keep working. The goal is to improve equipment and gain enough power to become the Potion Baroness.
